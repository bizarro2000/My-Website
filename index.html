About Me

Hailing from Los Angeles, CA, Joshua McLain is a 3D artist and developer who likes to focus on interactive design as well as usability. He first began his artist journey when he was a teen making comics, he then grew fond of game development and, most importantly, 3D modeling when he entered high school. Now as a Digital Technolgy and Culture major at Washington State University, he is pursuing this line of work to the fullest extent. Diving deep into the heart of his passion and creating worlds and telling stories that being him fulfillment.

Projects

Yuffie from Final Fantasy 7 Remake | 6-15-2024 | Blender/Substance Painter

This past summer, I played Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth. I fell in love with the game so much that I wanted to recreate one of my favorite characters I did this by first researching the production pipleline Square Enix used for its games. While Blender was not part of it, they did use Substance Painter for the texturing. So I modeled Yuffie from scratch using everything that I've learned. It was a long and grueling process which took two weeks. But I did it!
